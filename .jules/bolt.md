## 2024-06-30 - Missing Indices in SQLite OR Queries
**Learning:** SQLite's `OR` conditions can lead to full table scans even if only some of the columns in the `OR` expression have indices. In our codebase, queries like `WHERE tmdb_id IN (...) OR title_lower IN (...) OR show_title_lower IN (...)` were causing a full table scan because `tmdb_id` and `title_lower` were unindexed on the `watch_history` table. By adding indices for *all* columns involved in the `OR` clauses, SQLite can correctly utilize the `MULTI-INDEX OR` execution plan, converting the scan to multiple efficient index lookups.
**Action:** When inspecting query performance or writing new `OR` queries against SQLite, verify that indices exist for *every* column checked in the `OR` conditions; otherwise, the entire query falls back to scanning the whole table.
